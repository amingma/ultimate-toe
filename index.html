Ultimate Tic Tac Toe Rules

  • Board Structure
    • The game is played on a 3x3 grid of tic-tac-toe boards, making a total of 9 smaller boards
    • Each smaller board contains 9 cells, similar to a traditional tic-tac-toe board
  • Objective
    • The goal is to win three of the smaller boards in a row, column, or diagonal, which wins you the overall game
  • Gameplay
    • The first player can place their mark on any square on any of the smaller tic-tac-toe boards
    • After the first move, the cell chosen by the first player determines which smaller board the next player must play in
    • For example, if Player 1 places their mark in the top-right cell of any smaller board, Player 2 must place their mark in the top-right smaller board
    • If the designated smaller board is already won or full, the next player can play in any remaining open cell on any smaller board
  • Winning
    • The game ends in a player's victory when they have won three smaller boards in a row, column, or diagonal
    • If all smaller boards are full without possible moves left, then the game ends in a draw
